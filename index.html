Product Introduction

A first-aid kit that thinks for you.

MEDTECH is a digital emergency tool engineered specifically for travel medical kits. In a critical accident — a car crash, a remote-trail injury, or a workplace incident — any bystander can retrieve the patch and "peel and stick" it onto the victim's skin.

The patch instantly digitizes vital signs and streams them in real time to emergency responders. By providing the medical insight an ambulance team would normally only see on arrival, MEDTECH ensures that every second of the Golden Hour is spent treating — not diagnosing.

Key Functions & Sensors

Five medical-grade sensors.
One flexible PCB.

Powered by Flexible Electronics technology, the patch integrates a complete clinical-grade sensor stack into a single conformable surface — thin enough to wear, robust enough to trust.

Real-time ECG & Heart Rate

Captures electrical heart signals and pulse frequency to identify cardiac arrest the moment it begins.

Blood Pressure Estimation

Advanced optical PPG sensors track blood pressure trends, providing early warning for internal bleeding or shock.

Respiratory Rate & SpO₂

Measures breaths per minute and blood oxygen saturation to identify airway obstructions or lung trauma.

Body Temperature

Continuously tracks core body temperature to detect hypothermia, heat stroke, or infection-induced fever.

GPS / GNSS Positioning

A built-in low-power satellite module transmits precise coordinates to rescue teams — even in remote areas where cellular signal fails.

Step-by-Step Usage

Engineered for zero-friction use.

No medical training. No app setup. No hesitation. Four steps from patch to paramedic.

  1. 01

    Peel Off

    Retrieve the patch from your travel med-kit. Peeling the protective film automatically activates the built-in flexible battery — no buttons, no pairing.

  2. 02

    Apply

    Stick the patch firmly onto clean skin — ideally the chest or upper arm. The medical-grade adhesive holds secure even in rain, sweat, or rough terrain.

  3. 03

    Monitor

    Sensors immediately begin streaming. Vital signs and GPS coordinates sync via Bluetooth or satellite uplink directly to the receiving hospital's dashboard.

  4. 04

    Rescue

    Paramedics arrive already informed. Real-time data on their tablets allows them to triage and treat the most critical victims first — every second counts.
